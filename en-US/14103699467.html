Graphics Editor

With the appropriate security rights, you can create and modify simple as well as complex graphical representations of your devices, facility, campus, or equipment using the Graphics Editor. These graphical representations can contain dynamic elements to represent devices or values you want to monitor or control. You can also test and simulate your dynamic graphics before going online with them.

Graphics, Symbols, and graphic templates are all configured in Engineering mode. For related procedures or workflows , see the step-by-step section.
