Completing the Validation Dialog When Four Eyes Is Enabled

  1. Four Eyes authentication is enabled.
  2. You have made changes to a validated object, and the Validation Required dialog box displays.
  • Do one of the following:
    • For objects with a validation profile of Monitored, have a supervisor with privileges enter his or her user name and password. Then click OK.
    • For objects with a validation profile of Enabled, have a supervisor with privileges enter his or her user name and password, and then enter or select a comment that explains the reason for the change. Then click OK.
    • For objects with a validation profile of Supervised, enter your user password, and then have a supervisor with privileges enter his or her user name and password. Enter or select a comment that explains the reason for the change, and then click OK.
  1. The system records the changes in the Log Viewer.
