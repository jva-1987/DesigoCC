Hanwha Samsung SSM Compatibility and System Limits

Compatibility

  • SSM Version 2.10.4 (Desigo CC V4.0, V4.1, V4.2, and V5.0)
  • SSM Version 2.10.8 (Desigo CC V5.0 and higher)

System Limits

  • Maximum number of Hanwha Samsung SSM video management systems connected locally or remotely: 1

For other video limits, see Video Architectures and Limits.

Other Limitations

  • Camera diagnostics (normal, faulty, and so on) is not available.
