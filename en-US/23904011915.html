Assign a Journaling Printer to a Journaling Definition

You can assign only those journaling printers that are mapped to a template.

  1. Select Project > System Settings > Journaling > [journaling definition].
  2. In the Journaling Configurator tab, open the General Settings expander.
  3. From the Printer drop-down list, select a journaling printer.
