Desigo Automation

The Desigo CC management platform supports Desigo building automation and control (Desigo Automation) subsystems. The management platform contains a communication driver to connect to automation stations on the network. During the course of integrating your Desigo Automation in Desigo CC, you define one or more networks to allow imported data to successfully display in the System Browser tree.

This section includes background information on integrating and configuring Desigo CC with Desigo Automation. For related procedures and workflows, see the step-by-step section.
