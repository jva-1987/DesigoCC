Include Scripts Functions

The functions include and Collect allow including other scripts for reusing them. It is possible to include scripts that are already instantiated or scripts from a library. It is necessary to provide a relative path and the search will be performed in the project folder or in the library\ folders, depending on the passed parameters. The search in library folders follows the customization level order: the script will be searched first in the L4-Project, and then at L3-Country, L2-Region, and L1-Headquarter library levels.
