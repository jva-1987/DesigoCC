Setting Notifier AM Configuration Through Online Engineering

Scenario

The adapter is connected to the Notifier AM system, and you want to override online data with new configuration data. For background information, see Notifier AM Configuration.

Prerequisites

  • After installation, the NotifierAM_AdapterConfigurator.exe executable file is available in AdditionalSW\CEI_NotifierAM_Configurator locally or over the network.
  • The Notifier AM adapter is running and connected to the Notifier AM control panels. For instructions, see Installing and Starting the Notifier AM Adapter.
