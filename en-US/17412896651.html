BACnet Editor

The BACnet Object Editor allows you to browse, edit, and delete a wide range of BACnet objects in the management station. It also allows you to create new notification class and event enrollment objects. BACnet objects are accessed in the System Browser through the Management View (Field Networks > BACnet Network) or a User-defined View. For related procedures or workflows, see the step-by-step section.
