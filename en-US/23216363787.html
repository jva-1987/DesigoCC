Slide a Branch or Connection Handle

You can move a Branch or Connection handle along a pipe segment, or you can move an entire branch segment.

TIP: For greater precision, from the View tab disable the Snap option.

  1. You have a pipe segment on the canvas that has a branch.
  1. To slide a Branch or Connection handle:
    • Click on the Branch or Connection handle and drag it along the segment.
  2. To slide an entire pipe branch segment that is not merged with another pipe segment:
    • On the keyboard, press CTRL + E and then select the Branch handle and drag along the pipe segment until desired location reached.
