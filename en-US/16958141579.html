Display Room Information

Prerequisites:

  • System Manager is in Operation mode.
  • In System Browser, Logical View is selected. BIM graphics can also be selected in the Application View as an alternative.

 

Room Alarm

  1. Select Logical > [Subsystem name] > [Building name] > [Floor] > [Room].
  2. The BIM Viewer tab opens.
  3. The room is highlighted in the floor view.
  4. Click Show room alarms .
  5. Click Show room view .
  6. The selected room opens. The alarm is displayed on the floor per the alarm category (colors in the alarm overview).
  7. Click Show related alarms in EventList .
  8. The event list is filtered and displayed with the floor alarms.
  9. Acknowledge and reset alarm.
  10. Click Close event list .
  11. Click Show floor view .
  1. The room view is displayed in this example with alarm category Low (blue).

 

Highlight Individual Equipment

  1. Select Logical > [Subsystem name] > [Building name] > [Floor] > [Room].
  2. The BIM Viewer tab opens.
  3. The room is highlighted in the floor view.
  4. Click Show room view .
  5. The selected room opens.
  6. Click Highlight selected object .
  1. The last selected equipment is highlighted in color.

 

Highlight All Equipment

  1. Select Logical > [Subsystem name] > [Building name] > [Floor] > [Room].
  2. The BIM Viewer tab opens.
  3. The room is highlighted in the floor view.
  4. Click Show room view .
  5. The selected room opens.
  6. Click Highlight equipment of current room .
  1. All equipment is highlighted in color.

 

Display Data Sheet

  1. The URL links in the BIM data are correctly assigned to the objects.
    NOTE: Incorrectly assigned URL links must be corrected in the BIM data. URL links cannot be edited in Desigo CC.
  2. An Internet connection is required to open the data sheet via the URL link to a supplier.
  3. You must have the appropriate user rights at the supplier.
  1. Select Logical > [Subsystem name] > [Building name] > [Floor] > [Room].
  2. The BIM Viewer tab opens.
  3. The room is highlighted in the floor view.
  4. Click Show room view .
  5. The selected room opens.
  6. Click equipment in the room, for example, a room operator unit.
  7. Click Display the equipment's data sheet .
  8. Displays a list of available data sheets.
  9. Click the data sheet in the desired language.
  10. The data sheet displays in BIM Viewer.
  11. Click Download or Print to save the information.
