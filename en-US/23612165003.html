Reflect a Change of Your User Role

  1. You were assigned multiple roles.
  2. In the current work session, a message box informs you that a change to your user role assignment has been applied.
  1. In the message box, click OK.
  2. You are logged off. Desigo CC logon dialog box displays.
  3. Enter your username and password.
  4. Click Logon.
