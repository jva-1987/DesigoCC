Integrating Notifier AM

Scenario

You want to configure Desigo CC to operate on the Notifier AM systems. For background information, see Notifier AM Integration.

Prerequisites

  • The Notifier AM extension is installed and included in the active project. The following extensions are also selected automatically:
    • Fire Domain Libraries Common
    • Fire Domain Libraries EN
    • SORIS Driver
  • The Notifier AM adapter is running and connected to the Notifier AM control panels. For instructions, see Installing and Starting the Notifier AM Adapter.
  • The Notifier AM configuration is set. For instructions, see Working with Notifier AM Configurator.
  • System Manager is in Engineering mode.
  • System Browser is in Management View.

Licensing

This extension is covered by license. Check that the required license is available; otherwise, you may not be able to configure and work with this extension.
