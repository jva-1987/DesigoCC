Recipients

A recipient is a person or a group that is notified by sending a message (text, audio, and other file devices) from Notification.

Recipient users represent persons who will receive messages from Notification. Recipient user profiles contain personal contact information, information about one or more personal communication devices. For example, a recipient user can be reached through various types of personal communication devices such as cell phones (phone call and SMS) and email.
