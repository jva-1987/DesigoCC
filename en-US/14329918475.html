Multimedia Collection V8

The Multimedia Collection extension provides the possibility to configure and play back different media files (MP3 for audio files, MP4 for videos, and HTML pages with videos).

A default media collection, containing media files about the different disciplines (Mass Notification, Energy and Power Reporting, Room Automation, and the mobile app) is deployed with the Multimedia Collection extension. In addition, new multimedia collections can be created for specific purposes.
