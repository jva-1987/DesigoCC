Deleting the Configuration of a Location

  1. In System Browser, select Project > Field Networks > [SORIS network] > [Meteoblue adapter] > Locations > [location].
  2. In the Operation tab, next to the Configuration property, click Change and then click Send.
  4. Select [Meteoblue adapter].
  5. In the Extended Operation tab, next to the URL property, click Discover.
  1. The adapter configuration is refreshed to reflect the changes.
