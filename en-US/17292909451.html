Graphics Viewer Overview

The Graphics Viewer is the component of the Graphics application that allows you to view the graphics representing your facility or equipment. It is where you can change the current state of an object’s properties from a graphic, by using the floating Status and Commands windows. You can filter your view of a graphic by discipline, section, or you can zoom in and out for greater detail or for a birds-eye overview.
