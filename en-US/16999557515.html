SCAME S81-HS Events

The following is the list of the events available in Desigo CC for the SCAME S81-HS unit:

  • Panel Faults
  • Panel Cards Faults
  • Panel Connection Faults
  • Actuator Activation
  • Actuator Tamper
  • Actuator Exclusion
  • Actuator Anomalies and Faults
  • Detector Alarm
  • Detector PreAlarm
  • Detector Tamper
  • Detector Exclusion
  • Detector Anomalies and Faults
  • Rule Not Verified

    NOTE: In case of multiple Panel Faults or Panel Cards Faults occurring at the same time, the system will only show the two most important faults, plus the possible Card Fault.
