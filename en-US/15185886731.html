Upgrade the Management Station

Once you have

- verified and backed up the existing installation,

- obtained and verified the software distribution and

- completed the upgrade planning requirements,

you can upgrade the Server/Client/FEP Station using Custom mode.

 

During the upgrade of an existing management station software and its extensions, you can also add new installation languages, new extensions and new language packs.
