Overview of Notification

Notification is a notification system that uses audio, text, and other file contents to provide information and instructions to people who may be impacted by an emergency event.
An emergency event is any undesired situation that may cause significant disruptions to people, such as interruption of services, property damage, personal injuries, or even deaths. Examples of emergency events are:

  • Hazardous material: chemical spill, oil spill, radiation leak, toxic gas leak, gas explosion
  • Human-related: civil disturbance
  • Technology and infrastructure: communication failure, power failure, water failure, transportation failures and accidents

An operator needs to respond to these events by initiating and managing Incidents, which are used to manage and keep track of the notifications that were launched as part of these incidents.

Notifications such as messages are launched by the operator and are addressed to recipients such as users or groups to inform them on emergency events.
