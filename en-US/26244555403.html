Overview of PQ Advisor

Power Quality refers to the characteristics of the electrical power supplied to the electrical devices and their compatibility with these characteristics. Power Quality is how well electrical power meets the requirements of the devices that consume it. High power quality can be considered when the supply is consistent with a smooth sinusoidal wave of voltage at a specified magnitude and frequency. Poor power quality can lead to various problems, including inefficiency, equipment malfunction, or damage.

Powermanager analyses below fields:

  • Voltage variations
  • Frequency variations
  • Flicker severity
  • Voltage unbalance
  • Voltage THD
  • Harmonic violation
  • Voltage events
