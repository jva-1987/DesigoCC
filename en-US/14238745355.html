Troubleshooting Desigo Insight Migrations

Handling Log Entries

The window displays a Summary Log in the Desigo Graphics Migration tab with information, warnings, and errors that occurred during migration.

Log entries are displayed in the following colors:

  • Graphic start and end: Black
  • Information: Dark grey (or the color that you defined in the Text group editor in the Color column)
  • Warning: Orange
  • Error: Red

Click Show Log in the Desigo Graphics Migration tab to open the Summary Log window.
