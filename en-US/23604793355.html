PAC3200

The PAC3200 is a power monitoring device for displaying all the relevant line parameters. It collects all the basic measured variables and the extended measured variables (for example, THD). Limit values are available for line monitoring. It performs single-phase, two-phase, or three-phase measurement and can be used in two-wire, three-wire, or four-wire TN, TT, and IT systems. PAC3200 with multi-range power supply can thus be connected direct in every low-voltage system up to a line voltage of 690 V.
