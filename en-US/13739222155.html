Multimedia Collection

This section provides information for configuring the Multimedia Collection extension module, which extends the core functionality of Desigo CC by adding the capability to configure and play back different media files (MP3 for audio files, MP4 for videos, and HTML pages with videos). For operator information and instructions, see the more operating resources section.
