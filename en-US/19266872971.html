Correlated workflows

Workflow icons are organized into category folders and some of these folders may be related to each other.

By default, this visual element is not present but it is possible to customize the workflows acting on the customization of the system libraries. When scrolling the mouse over a specific workflow, it will be highlighted in yellow and the related workflows will be highlighted in blue.
