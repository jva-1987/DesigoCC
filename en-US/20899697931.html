Building X Connector

This section provides background information about Building X Connector. For procedures or workflows, see the step-by-step section.

The Building X Connector extension provides connectivity from Desigo CC installations to Building X applications such as Operations Manager, where you can remotely monitor the status of your site.

There are two ways to access and manage the data provided by the Building X Connector:

  • Through the Building X application. This method requires a process of export, discovery and upload of user-selected local devices and points, using the Desigo CC management station.
  • Through the Desigo CC Flex Client, which can be configured within SMC.
