Select a Workstation and Driver

  1. From the Station Selection drop-down list, select the workstation your subsystem will be connected to.
  2. From the Driver selection drop-down list, do one of the following:
    • If you select a new driver, click Next page . The wizard displays Step 4 of 5. Go to the Enter Driver Information topic.
    • If a SORIS driver already exists, it is selected by default. Click Next page . The wizard displays Step 5 of 5. Go to the Review Summary Information topic.
