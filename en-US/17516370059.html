Additional Advanced Reports for Pharma Procedures

Complete the following procedures as needed.

Customize the Pharma List Series Text Group

  1. You would like to modify the Pharma List Series text group so that it matches the number of trend series in the Trend Chart Report.
  1. Select Project > System Settings > Libraries > L1-Headquarter > BA > Software > Pharma Reporting > Texts.
  2. Select Pharma List Series.
  3. In the Text Group Editor tab, click Customize .
  4. Click OK.
  1. The Pharma List Series text group is cloned under the allowed customization level and can now be modified.
