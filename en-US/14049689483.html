Desigo Automation

Desigo building automation and control (Desigo Automation) provides flexible and energy-optimized automation solutions for the room at maximum comfort. The freely programmable PXC3, PXC4, PXC5 as well as the DXR automation station with preconfigured applications are ideal to automate heating, ventilation, and A/C plants as well as lighting and shading in a room.

This section includes background information on integrating and configuring Desigo CC with Desigo Automation.

For related procedures and workflows, see the step-by-step section.
