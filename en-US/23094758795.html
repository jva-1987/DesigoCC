Create Managed Meters

  1. Select Applications > Energy and Power > Managed Meters.
  2. Click the Meter Management tab.
  3. The Meter Management tab refreshes and displays the managed meter fields in the Managed Meter Configuration expander.
  4. Enter the information in the fields in the General, Classification, and Meter Synchronization sections in Managed Meters Configuration.
  5. Click Save .
  6. The managed meter is created and is historized by default to January 1, 1970.
