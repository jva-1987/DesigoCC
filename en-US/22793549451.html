Configure the General Settings of the Operating Procedure

You must specify the general parameters of an operating procedure.

  1. Select Project > System Settings > Operating Procedures > […] > [operating procedure].
  2. In the Operating Procedure tab, open the General Settings expander.
  3. Enter values in the following fields:
    • Priority
    • Steps Execution Type
    • On new occurrence/If happens after
    • Force manual close
    • Keep primary event
    • Notes
