Powermanager Graphics

Graphics is an integrated application in powermanager which is used to represent devices/objects graphically to monitor specific object properties. Users can create graphics by just dragging objects onto the graphic page, without manually binding objects to graphical symbols. With graphic editor, it is even possible to modify the configurations of device symbols if required and save locally.

Below are the list of symbol used in powermanager and their details:
