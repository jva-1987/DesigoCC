Create a Trend View Definition from Related Items

  1. Navigate to the Related Items tab and select New Trend .
  2. Trend View opens a secondary view.
  3. Change the Trend View properties and the properties for each series.
    For more information, see Configuring Trend View Definition.
  4. Click Save .
