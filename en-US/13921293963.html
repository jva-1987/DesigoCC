Desigo Automation V8

The Desigo Automation extension supports the integration of an entire building. This includes air handling units, heating and AC plants as well as multiple room automation discipline (such as HVAC, lighting, shading) that are combined into a comprehensive solution.

Desigo Automation supports automation stations and devices developed with ABT Site:

  • PXC3, PXC4, PXC5, PXC7
  • DXR1, DXR2
  • Smart Thermostat
  • iValve (Intelligent valves)
