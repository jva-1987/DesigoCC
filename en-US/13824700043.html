Drawing a Polygon Element

The polygon element consists of one closed figure with three or more line segments.

  1. You are in Design or Test mode and have a graphic open.
  1. On the Home tab, in the Elements group, click Polygon .
  2. The mouse changes to a cross-hair cursor.
  3. Click the canvas and drag until the desired polygon is created, and then release the mouse button.
  1. A polygon element displays on the canvas.
