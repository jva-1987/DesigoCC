Aguilera AESA Cx System Architecture

The Aguilera AE/SA-Cx algorithmic central unit can communicate with the management station over the Modbus TCP protocol via the AE/SA-GAT communication interface, which works as a bridge between the Modbus and the algorithmic system protocols used by the AE/SA-C2, AE/SA-C8, AE/SA-C23H and AE/SA-C83H algorithmic central units.
