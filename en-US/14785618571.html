CEI-ABI Compatibility and System Limits

Compatibility

  • CEI 795
  • Desigo CC V3.0 SR3 or higher

The CEI-ABI adapter has been successfully tested with CS9 intrusion panel. Compatibility with any other CEI-ABI intrusion panel must be evaluated by verifying the CEI-ABI interoperability table of the control panel.
