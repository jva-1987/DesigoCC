Synova FC700A System Limits and Compatibility

System Limits

  • 1 NK823x per BACnet network
  • 4 fire systems (FG700A) per NK823x (depending on NK823x hardware features), and max. 50 fire subsystems (FT700A, FC700A, FG700A) per NK823x
  • 16 subsystems (FT700A, FC700A, FG700A) per fire system
  • 2 FG700A gateways per fire system

Legacy DMS Connectivity Limits

 

Maximum

Configured objects per NK823x

25,000 (note 1)

NOTES

  1. In DMS configurations, 25,000 configured objects correspond to about 10,000 physical points (detectors, actuators, and so on).

Compatibility Information

  • Panel firmware / tool version: 762 / SWE700A V1.35.0-000.
