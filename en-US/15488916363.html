KNX Troubleshooting

This section covers several areas of troubleshooting.

Troubleshooting SORIS

In SORIS, see SORIS Troubleshooting.

Troubleshooting KNX Adapter

In case of issues with the KNX adapter, do the following:

  1. Start the adapter from console:
    • In C:\Program Files (x86)\Siemens\KNX Adapter\, double-click the executable Siemens.Gms.KNXAdapter.exe.
  2. View the KNX adapter logs in the console, such as telegrams (for example, adapter initialization, connection to the driver, any change of value for properties, and commands sent to KNX devices) and warnings/errors, if any (for example, The IA requested for this connection is in use).
