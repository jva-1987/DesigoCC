Setting Notifier AM Configuration Through Offline Engineering

Scenario

The adapter is not yet connected to the Notifier AM system, and you want to adjust configuration in offline mode by loading the configuration file, and adjusting the settings. For background information, see Notifier AM Configuration.

Prerequisites

  • After installation, the NotifierAM_AdapterConfigurator.exe executable file is available in AdditionalSW\CEI_NotifierAM_Configurator locally or over the network.
  • The Notifier AM adapter is running and connected to the Notifier AM control panels. For instructions, see Installing and Starting the Notifier AM Adapter.
