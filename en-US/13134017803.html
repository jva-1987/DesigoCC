Integrating IEC61850 Power Meters into the Management Platform

Scenario
You want to integrate IEC61850 power meters into the management platform.
For background information, see the reference section.

Prerequisites

  • You have the software distribution containing the IEC61850 and IEC61850 Power Devices 2.
  • You have installed IEC61850 and IEC61850 Power Devices 2 and added them to a management station project using the SMC.
  • System Browser is in Management View.
