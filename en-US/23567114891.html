Modify a Management Station Calendar

  1. Select Applications > Schedules > Management Station Calendars.
  2. Open the calendar you want to update. Do one of the following:
    • To modify start dates, end dates, or recurrence settings, select a calendar entry from the Calendar Entries section, and select the settings you want.
    • To delete a calendar entry, click Delete .
      NOTE: If you click the Delete from the Scheduler toolbar, you will delete the entire calendar.
  3. Click Save .
