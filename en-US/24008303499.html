Analyze the Log Report

  1. Click Pause tracing .
    • Updating in the Information window is paused. However, the data continues to be logged in the background. Once pause is de-selected, any new information will display in the window.
  2. Scroll to the desired location to analyze the corresponding message.
  3. Click Resume file reading to display the most recent data in the Information window or click Pause tracing to pause the most recent data. Alternatively, click Stop tracing to stop data logging and click Resume tracing to resume data logging.

NOTE: Click Refresh from file to update the data also from the native PVSS_II.Log file. If you need to send information, select the range and press CTRL+C. Copy the selected information in a document or mail message by pressing CTRL+V.
