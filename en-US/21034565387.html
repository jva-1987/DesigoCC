Notifier AM System Architecture

The Notifier AM-2000Nm, AM-4000, and AM6000N fire control panels can communicate with the management station over the TCP/IP protocol through an RS232 to Ethernet converter, which enables data transmission for panels equipped with the SIB600 serial output interface board.
