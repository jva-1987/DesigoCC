Installing and Starting the Signify Adapter

Scenario

You want to connect Desigo CC to a Signify system. For background information, see the reference section.

Prerequisites

  • The PC on which you are installing the adapter software must have Internet access.
  • The SignifyAdapter_Setup.msi installer package, provided after installation in
    [Installation Drive]:\[Installation Folder]\GMSMainProject\AddSW\, is available locally or over the network.

After the upgrade of this extension, the adapter must be manually upgraded by running the adapter MSI file, and then selecting the upgrade option.
