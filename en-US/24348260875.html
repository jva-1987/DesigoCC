HikVision Compatibility and System Limits

Compatibility

  • Desigo CC V5.0 or higher
  • NVRs and DVRs compatible with HCNetSDK V6.1.9.4

Other Limitations

  • Video tracks can be played 4x (instead of 6x).
  • Tags will not be in synch with NVR.

For other video limits, see Video Architectures and Limits.
