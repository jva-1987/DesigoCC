Integrating the PQ Advisor

Scenario

You want to integrate the PowerQuality devices with the management platform.

For background information, see the reference section.

 

Prerequisites

  • You have installed IEC61850Adaptor and PowerQuality extension modules. Refer Install or Update an Extension.
  • You have added IEC61850Adaptor and PowerQuality extension modules to your project. Refer (Optional) Add to or Remove an Extension from a Project.
  • You have license for IEC 61850, and PowerQuality, and activated it.
  • You have integrated IEC 61850 devices. Refer Integrating the IEC 61850 Devices.
  • You have created a Web Services Application.
