Client Profile for Display-Only Clients (KIOSK)

The KIOSK Client Profile sets a special user interface with display-only capabilities. It is intended for Building Automation installations where Desigo CC stations are located in public or generally accessible places. For instructions see Setting Up a KIOSK Station in Building Automation Profiles.
