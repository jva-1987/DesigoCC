To….

  • Slide handle without changing branch angle. (Branch handle) 

CTRL + E + Drag

Extend the segment with a restricted 450/900 angle

Snap Enabled + Drag

*Access Snap checkbox from the Ribbon > View tab. Uncheck to disable.

Applies to: Start, End, Midpoint, and Vertex handles.

Applies to: Start and End handles.

Merge with another segment or Pipe element.
