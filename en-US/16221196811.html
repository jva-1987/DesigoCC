Reports

Powermanager offers a wide range of reports that help in analyzing parameters, such as energy, power demand, and cost centers. These reports are generated from values that are polled from the devices or values derived from them. These reports can be used to analyze, plan and forecast.

The main function of the Powermanager reports application is to create, view, and configure reports, using the report templates. Once created, you can execute a report for the configured Powermanager device, view it as a PDF or as an XLSX. Furthermore, you can also print a report or schedule a report execution and send it as an email or save in a folder.
