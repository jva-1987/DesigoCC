Integrating March Networks Video Surveillance System

To integrate the March Networks video surveillance system in Desigo CC, you must follow the main integration workflow, Integrating Video Surveillance, located in the Video engineering step-by-step section, with the variants specified here.

Main Configuration Workflow

  1. In Set Up Video Devices and the VMS, follow the instructions as given.
    To install the VMS server, see Installing the Provider for March Networks Video Surveillance System.
  2. In Configure Project Settings for Video, follow the instructions as given completing the information for the March Networks provider. See Setting the Provider for March Networks Video Surveillance System.
  3. In Set up User Accounts and Networking for Video, do not perform the instructions given, which are for the Milestone/Siveillance VMS. Instead, perform the following: Configuring Video Export for March Networks Video Surveillance System.
  4. Complete Acquire the Video Configuration from the VMS as given.
