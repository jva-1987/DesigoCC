PAC1600

The PAC1600 is a family of E-counters for measuring consumption values in three-phase systems. This unit registers exported or imported energy and it stores active and reactive energy. It is a measuring device for measuring the basic electrical variables in low voltage power distribution. Measured variables are shown on the display of the PAC1600.

The PAC1600 is mounted on a DIN rail or is screwed in place using extractable clips.
