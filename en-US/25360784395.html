Configuring PowerQuality Alarms

If PowerQuality alarms are missing from alarms section at top, then perform below steps to add PowerQuality Alarms.

  1. You have added IEC 61850 and PowerQuality extension modules to your project.
  2. System manager is in Engineering mode.
  3. In System Browser, Management View is selected.
  1. Navigate to SystemSettings > Libraries > HQ > Global > Global_Events_HQ_1 > Schemas > PM.
  2. Click Activate .

  3. Close Powermanager.
  4. Navigate to SMC, and restart the project.
  5. Open Powermanager and navigate to Management View > SystemSettings > Security > Security tab > Event Rights expander > Power Quality.

  6. Click Save .
  7. Exit and reopen Powermanager.
  1. PowerQuality Alarms configured successfully and are available in alarm section at the top.
