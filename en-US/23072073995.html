Notifier AM Symbols

When integrating the Notifier AM extension, the following symbols are available in graphics:

DYN_All_FireDomain_Command_DisableEnable_Central_001_150

  • Default symbol for panel, detector, output, and zone graphic commands (automatic and manual).
  • It is a toggle button that displays Disable or Enable commands depending on the current point status.

 

DYN_All_FireDomain_Command_ActivateDeactivate_Central_001_150

  • Default symbol for panel and output graphic commands (automatic and manual).
  • It is a toggle button that displays Deactivate or Activate commands depending on the current point status.
