Acknowledge or Reset an SPC Panel

From the Extended Operation you can issue an acknowledge or reset command to an SPC panel. The command will be applied to all the intrusion zones and doors under that panel.

  1. Select Project > Field Networks > [SPC Network] > [SPC panel].
  2. In the Extended Operation tab, next to the Mode property, click on the required command:
    • Acknowledge.
    • Reset.

NOTE: These command buttons always appear undimmed, even if there are no currently active events on the panel.
