AlgoRex CS11 System Limits and Compatibility

System Limits

Legacy DMS Connectivity Limits

 

Maximum

NK823x adapter per BACnet network

1

Downstream serial connections per NK823x

4 (note 1)

Subsystems (CC11, CI11, CK11, STT11, STT20, SI400 Sintony, DF8000) per NK823x

50

Subsystems (CC11, CI11, CK11, STT11, FC20, FT20, STT20) per Cerloop via MK7022/NK823x

40

C-Bus stations (CT11, CC11, CI11, CK11) per AlgoRex fire system

16

CK11 gateways per AlgoRex fire system (8 CC11/CI11 panels per CK11)

2

NOTES

  1. Up to 4 downstream connections per NK823x, on the 5 possible communication lines: the 4 COM ports and the I2C line for DF8000. A configuration including all five connections is not supported.
