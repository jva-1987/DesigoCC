Exporting Graphics

  1. You have prepared graphics for export.
  1. Log on to the console as the sicx user.
  2. Run the siclimat_export_repo --graphics script.
  3. The graphics data is exported to the graphics subdirectory of the repository, with a subsequent subdirectory for each OS.
  4. (Optional) To export additional graphics, prepare the graphics for export and then repeat steps 1 and 2.
