Adding Smart Thermostat to Floor Plan

Scenario: You have integrated your room units and want to add them to the floor plan.

Prerequisites:

  • System Manager is in Engineering mode.
  • In System Browser, Logical View is selected.
  • The floor plan on a floor or room is created and saved.

 

Steps:

 

  1. Select Logical > [Hierarchy name] > [Hierarchy level 1-n] > [Room].
  2. Displays the Default tab.
  3. Drag the selected data point to the desired X/Y position.
  4. The Smart Thermostat symbol is placed on the graphics page.
  5. Click Save .
