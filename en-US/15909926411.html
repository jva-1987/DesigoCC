SecuriFire BACnet Events

The following is the list of events available in Desigo CC for SecuriFire panels:

  • Panel Deactivated
  • Zone Fire Alarm
  • Zone Fault
  • Zone Disabled
  • Zone Activation
  • Zone Active
  • Zone Revision
  • Zone Hard Alarm
  • Zone Prealarm
  • Zone Presignal
  • Zone Pollution Alarm
  • Zone Pollution
  • Zone Silent Alarm
  • Zone Disabled Internal
  • Zone Revision
  • Extinguishing Zone Extinguishing Alarm
  • Extinguishing Zone Fault
  • Extinguishing Zone Disablement
  • Extinguishing Zone Activation
  • Extinguishing Zone Active
  • Extinguishing Zone Revision
  • Extinguishing Zone Revision Operation
  • Input/Output Alarm
  • Input/Output Fault
  • Input/Output Disabled
  • Input/Output Activation
  • Input/Output Active
  • Input/Output Revision
  • Detector Alarm
  • Detector Fault
  • Detector Disabled
  • Detector Activation
  • Detector Active
  • Detector Revision
  • Detector State/Substate events (refer to text table TxG_SecuriFire_Detector_StateSubstate_150)

Combined events

SecuriFire objects support combined conditions (such as, Alarm + Fault). Accordingly, the Desigo CC integration also provides combined event texts to cover these cases, although not specified in the list above.
