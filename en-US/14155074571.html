Zooming and Panning

The Graphics Editor supports zooming and panning within the active canvas. Zooming allows you to magnify the canvas image, and panning allows you to move the center of the Canvas view. The Zoom and Pan commands are accessed from the Home tab in the Zoom group. The Panning or Zooming modes are automatically deactivated when you select a new drawing object or when a new or existing graphic page is opened.

You can use the features from the Zoom group or, you can use the keyboard and mouse buttons to zoom and pan in, out, and around your graphics.
