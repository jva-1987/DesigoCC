Configure the General Settings for the Step

You must specify the general parameters of an operating procedure step.

  1. Select Project > System Settings > Operating Procedures > […] > [operating procedure] > [step].
  2. In the Operating Procedure tab, open the General Settings expander.
  3. Specify the following settings:
    • Repeatable
    • Mandatory
  4. (Optional) In the Notes field, enter any additional information.
