Silencing the Audio Alert

The buzzer on the management station sounds to alert you to a new event. You can mute the audio alert temporarily or permanently.

 

Temporarily Mute the Audio Alert

  1. The audio alert buzzer on a Desigo CC station is sounding, and you want to mute it temporarily.
  • In the Summary bar, click Audio Alert .
  1. The icon changes to muted and the sound stops, even if there are still unacknowledged events. Muting applies only to the pre-existing events: the audio alert will still sound for any new events that come in.
  • To manually unmute the audio alert, click Audio Alert again.

Permanently Silence the Audio Alert

Depending on the client profile, you can permanently disable the audio alert buzzer on a Desigo CC station, so that it does not sound even if new events come in.

  • In the Summary bar, right-click Audio Alert .
  1. The icon changes to disabled .

 

  • To manually re-enable the audio alert, right-click Audio Alert again.
