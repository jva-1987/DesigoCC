Recommended workflows

Workflow icons are organized into category folders and some of these folders are marked with pin icon .

By default, the workflows marked with the pin icon are those strongly recommended, through which you launch work procedures that are indispensable in the system configuration. It is therefore likely that the activity you are looking for is within a recommended workflow.

NOTE: By acting on the customization of the system libraries it is possible to choose which workflows are marked with the pin icon.
