Use Mouse Zoom

The zoom function allows you to select a time range in the currently selected Trend View
(applies as well for the comparison view) by zooming in on the X-axis and Y-axis.

  1. You are in an active Trend View and in the Stop mode.
  1. Click Zoom .
  2. Navigate to the left zoom starting range.
  3. The point changes shape and the tooltip displays the current position.
  4. Drag the pointer to the right zoom end range.
  5. The selected zoom range displays in the Trend View.
  6. Click Zoom .
  1. The original time range, prior to the zoom in, displays, even if you zoom multiple times.
