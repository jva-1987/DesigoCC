Progress Verification

  1. The incident to be initiated was created under Notification > Incident Templates.
  2. The notification to be launched is available under Notification > Notification Templates.
  3. System Manager is in Operating mode.
  1. In System Browser, select Application View.
  2. Select an incident template or select a notification template.
  3. The Initiate Incident tab displays.
  4. Check the status of the Progress bar and do one of the following:
    • If the progress has reached 100 percent, click Initiate.
    • If the progress is less than 100 percent, check for in other fields and enter the missing data in the appropriate fields.
      NOTE: represents missing data in mandatory fields.
  5. The status of the Progress bar turns to 100 percent once the required data in all mandatory fields has been entered.
  6. Click Initiate.
  1. The corresponding Incident is initiated.
