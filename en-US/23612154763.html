Do an Operator Log Off

  1. You have appropriate privileges to do an operator log off.
  2. The currently logged-on operator is not at the workstation, and you urgently need to log onto Desigo CC.
  • In the Summary bar, select Menu > Logoff.
  1. The currently logged-on operator is logged off from Desigo CC, and the logon dialog box displays.
