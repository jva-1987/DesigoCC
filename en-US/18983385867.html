Discovering the Adapter Configuration

  1. You created the SORIS driver, network, and adapter manually or using the network wizard. This is the first time FIAS adapter is configured, and it is necessary to manually discover the booked rooms.
  1. Select Project > Field Networks > [SORIS network] > [FIAS adapter].
  2. In the Extended Operation tab, the Online property indicates Connected while the Discovery Status property indicates Idle.
  3. Next to the URL property, click Discover.
  1. System Browser refreshes and displays any booked rooms under Hotel Rooms System.
