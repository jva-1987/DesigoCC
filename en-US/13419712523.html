Executing a Graphic Step in Assisted Treatment

  1. You are in the Assisted Treatment window and the operating procedure includes a graphic step that you want to do. For background information about the Desigo CC graphics feature, see the reference section.
  • From the Steps list, select the [graphic step].
  1. A graphic of the facility displays in the Default tab.
