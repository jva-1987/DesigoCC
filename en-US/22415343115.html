Run Auto Discovery

  1. You want to automatically discover devices only.
  1. Click the Discovery Settings expander.
  2. Check Enable auto-discovery for devices.
  3. Click Save .
  4. As devices are discovered, they display in the Discovery Status section.
  5. Start monitoring the discovery progress in the Discovery Status section, keeping in mind that devices are only discovered if they are on the network. If you do not see activity in the Discovery Status section or in the BACnet network in System Browser, you must troubleshoot the problem.
  6. To automatically stop discovering panels, uncheck Enable auto-discovery for devices.
