Configure Incident Templates

  1. Select Applications > Notification > Incident Templates > [incident template].
  2. The selected incident template displays in the Primary pane in the Incident Template Editor tab.
  3. Configure the general settings for the Incident Template in the General Settings expander.
  4. Associate a notification template to a launch group.
  5. (Optional) Create an Incident Trigger rule.
  6. The incident template is created and configured.
