Operation

The Operation tab allows you to display and change the current state of an object’s properties in your management platform. The Operation tab is designed for the most common daily tasks. Access to objects is based upon the object privileges and privilege profiles set for you by your system administrator.

For procedures or workflows, see the step-by-step section.
