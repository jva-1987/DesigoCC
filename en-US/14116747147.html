Creating Related Items

For consistent single-click operation, the most important plant information must be linked to a graphics page. Can occur as part of engineering or later at the user.

  1. The graphics page is completed and open.
  1. Click Configurator.
  2. In System Browser, select Manual Navigation.
  3. In System Browser, select Logical view.
  4. Select Logical > [Hierarchy structure] >
    • Scheduler (Sched)
    • Trend (Trend object)
    • Other Objects
      and drag the applicable nodes to the Related Items expander of the Object Configurator tab (not in the Related Items tab).
  5. Click Save .
  1. Objects are associated with the graphics page. The related items are displayed in the Related Items tab if the graphics page is selected. Selecting the related item opens the corresponding information in the Secondary pane.
