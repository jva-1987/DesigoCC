GAMMA Presence Detectors Graphic Templates

The following graphic templates are provided with this integration to graphically represent the GAMMA presence detectors.

TEM_KNX_PresenceDetector_None_001

In Engineering mode, this is the graphic template for the UP 258E22 and UP 258D12 presence detectors.

In Operating mode, this is the graphic for the UP 258E22 and UP 258D12 presence detector.

TEM_KNX_UP258Dxx_None_001

In Engineering mode, this is the graphic template for the UP 258D31, UP 258D41, UP 258D51, and UP 258D61 presence detectors.

In Operating mode, this is the graphic for the UP 258D31, UP 258D41, UP 258D51, and UP 258D61 presence detectors.
