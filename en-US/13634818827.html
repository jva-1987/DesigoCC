Overview of Views

In Engineering mode, authorized technicians can create and customize views to allow operators to browse the system in Operating mode by following the most suitable operating workflows.

Desigo CC supports several views that offer different ways to look at the system:

  • Management View
  • Application View
  • logical view
  • physical view
  • user-defined view

By default, Management View and Application View are available in System Browser. All other views become available only when they are configured.
