Powermanager Devices

Using the powermanager extension you can integrate power devices, power quality devices, circuit breakers, logical devices and any Modbus TCP-enabled measuring devices into the management station. For more information on Modbus TCP enabled measuring devices supported by powermanager, see Devices.

In Engineering mode, you can configure devices when you select an area or a sector.
