Shutting Down the System Before License Mode Expiration

  1. Your Desigo CC client is in Demo Mode or Courtesy Mode and the countdown timer is about to expire. You are informed by a dialog box that the Desigo CC client will shut down in 60 seconds: you cannot dismiss this dialog box. Even though you can continue your work (for example, saving data) until the system shuts down, you want to end your session immediately.
  • In the dialog box, click Shutdown Now.
  1. The Desigo CC client shuts down.
    NOTE: If the missing license features are detected again before the 60 seconds expire, the dialog box disappears and the license mode reverts to Normal).
