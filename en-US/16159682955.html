Use Own Edited Plant as a Reference Plant

  1. Functions from an edited plant are only taken over for editing after saving.
  1. Select Logical > [Hierarchy] > [Present edited plant].
  2. Drag the present plant for edit to the Reference plants expander.
  3. Click Automatically assign functions based on reference plants.
  4. The number of assigned functions are displayed.
  5. Click OK.
  6. Edited objects change the state from green to black.
  7. Click Save .
