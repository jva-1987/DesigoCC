Import Data Points

  1. Select a device.
  2. Click Objects.
  3. All data points of that device are listed.
  4. Select the data points you want to import.
  5. Click Save .
  6. Click Back and repeat the previous steps for each device you want to import.
  7. Click Import.
  1. The progress displays in the Discovery Status section. The data points display in System Browser under the selected network.
