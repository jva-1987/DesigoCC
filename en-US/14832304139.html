Selesta VAM Symbols

In addition to the standard symbols for doors and readers, when integrating the Selesta VAM extension, the following symbols are also available in graphics:

Area Symbols

DYN_All_Selesta_Area_Attendance_SingleValue_None_001

  • Default symbols for the Selesta attendance areas, specific for property information (employees, visitors, contractors, and vehicles).
  • The value in the symbols provides information about occupancy related to the chosen property of the area.

DYN_All_Selesta_Area_Attendance_AllValues_None_001

  • Default symbol for the Selesta attendance areas with complete occupancy information available.
  • The values in the symbol provide information on occupancy for the entire property of the area.
