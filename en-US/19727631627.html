Activating Highlighted Objects

To activate the highlighted objects in System Browser, do the following:

  1. To activate highlighted objects, do one of the following:
    • From the Toolbar: Click Highlight.
    • From the Graphics Editor Ribbon: Click Runtime.
  2. NOTE: Once either button is selected, the other button is selected as well.
  3. To deactivate highlighted objects, click the Highlight button, or from the Ribbon, select Design, or Test mode.
