Network Setup Wizard

The Network Wizard extends the core functionality of Desigo CC by adding the capability to automate the field networks engineering tasks with few configuration steps before importing device data.

Depending on the subsystem configuration, the number of wizard pages and their settings may vary. To find the subsystem-specific workflow, search for “network wizard” and select the corresponding link in the result list.
