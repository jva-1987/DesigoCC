Integrating Modbus TCP Power Devices into the Management Platform

Scenario
You want to integrate Modbus power devices 2 into the management platform.
For background information, see the reference section.

Prerequisites

  • You have the software distribution containing the Modbus TCP and Modbus TCP Power Devices 2.
  • You have installed Modbus TCP and Modbus TCP Power Devices 2 and added them to a management platform project using the SMC.
  • System Manager is in the Engineering mode.
  • System Browser is in Management View.
