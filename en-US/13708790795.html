Operating the Building

The view structure of a project is always dependent on the configuration but most projects include the following views from which the different building automation disciplines can be operated:

  • Topology page or an overview page
  • Air handling units
  • Room operation with light and blinds
  • Heating with boilers, domestic hot water and heating groups
  • Refrigeration machine and cooling groups.
