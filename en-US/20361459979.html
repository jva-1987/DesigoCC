GAMMA Sensors and Room Operator Units Graphic Templates

The following graphic templates are provided with this integration to graphically represent the GAMMA QMX3 sensors and room operator units.

In Engineering mode, this is the graphic template for the QMX3 Px0 room sensor.

In Operating mode, this is the graphic for the QMX3 Px0 room sensor.

In Operating mode, this is the graphic for the QMX3 Px2 room sensor.

TEM_KNX_QMX3_Px4_None_001

In Operating mode, this is the graphic for the QMX3 Px4 room sensor.

TEM_KNX_QMX3_Px7_None_001

In Engineering mode, this is the graphic template for the QMX3 Px7 room sensor.

In Operating mode, this is the graphic for the QMX3 Px7 room sensor.
