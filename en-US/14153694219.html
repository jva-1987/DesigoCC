8

Move the slider with your mouse to increase and decrease the magnification of the selected graphic template icon for viewing within the Graphic Templates Browser view. The magnified value displays as you move the slider and has a minimum magnification of 30% and a maximum of 300%.

The slider displays only when you are in the Thumbnail view.

In the Graphic Templates Browser’sList view, the graphic templates are listed by Name, Library, and File. A thumbnail preview is not displayed.
