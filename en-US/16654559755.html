Text and Memo

This section provides step-by-step instructions for Text and Memo tasks. For background information, see the reference section.

Modify a Memo

  1. You have selected an object and would like to modify the memo.
  1. Click the Text and Memo tab.
  2. Click , and then modify the memo as desired.
    NOTE: You can use standard Windows keyboard shortcuts—for example, CTRL+C for copy, CTRL+V for paste, and so on.
  3. Click .
  1. The memo is saved.

 

Copy Informational Text and Memo

  1. You have selected an object.
  1. Click the Text and Memo tab.
  2. Click to copy both the informational and memo text, which you can paste as a table into Word or Outlook.
