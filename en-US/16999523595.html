SCAME S81-HS Symbols

In addition to the standard symbols for fire objects, when integrating the SCAME S81-HS extension, the following symbols are also available in graphics:

DYN_3D_SCAME_Input_None_Horizontal_001_150

  • Non-default symbol for detector objects.

DYN_3D_SCAME_Output_None_Horizontal_001_150

  • Default symbol for actuator objects.

DYN_3D_SCAME_GasZone_None_Horizontal_002_150

  • Default symbol for gas zone objects.
