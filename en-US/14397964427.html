Advantronic AD300 System Architecture

The Advantronic analogic central units of the AD300 family can communicate to the management station over the Modbus TCP protocol via a specific communication interface, which works as a bridge between the Modbus protocol and the proprietary protocol of the Advantronic control units of the AD300 family.
