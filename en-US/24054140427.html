Displaying Automation Station Information on a Data Point

Scenario: You want to display an automation station belonging to a data point.

  1. In System Browser, select Logical view.
  2. Select Logical View > [Network Name] > [Plant Name] > [Data Point].
  3. In the Related Items, the corresponding automation station is displayed under Device.
  4. Click the displayed related item.
  1. The information on the automation station is displayed in the Secondary pane.
