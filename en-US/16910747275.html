Manually Overriding a Switch Group

  1. You want to override the fixture profile settings associated with a switch group so to dim the light of a floor temporarily.
  2. In System Browser, select Project > Field Networks > [SORIS network] > [Enlighted adapter] > [energy manager] > [campus] > [building] > [floor] > [SwitchGroups] > [switch group].
  • In the Extended Operation tab, next to the Mode property, enter the wanted period of time in minutes and click Manual Override.
