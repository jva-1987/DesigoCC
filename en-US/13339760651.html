Deleting Operating Procedures, Folders, or Steps

  1. System Manager is in Engineering mode.
  1. In System Browser, select Management View.
  2. Select Project > System Settings> Operating Procedures > [operating procedure] or [procedure step] or [folder].
  3. In the Operating Procedures tab, click Delete .
    NOTE: Deleting an operating procedures folder also deletes all the procedures and procedures steps inside it.
  4. A confirmation message displays.
  5. Click Yes.
  1. The operating procedure, procedure step, or folder is removed from System Browser.
