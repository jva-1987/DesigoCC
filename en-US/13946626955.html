Status and Command Propagation in System Browser

In the System Browser views, Desigo CC provides status and command propagation based on the parent-child model along the project hierarchy.

The status of common properties, such as alarm, fault, or technical exclusion, are relayed from source objects of the fire panel up to the top of the view.
Vice versa, the action of common commands is propagated down from high-level objects, where applicable along the hierarchical structure.

Referring to the internal structure of the fire panel, the propagation is implemented as follows:

  • The Detection tree propagation is supported to provide all required functionality
  • Control, Hardware and Operation trees are not supported, although certain statuses may be propagated
    NOTE: Propagation occurs in all trees for objects whose Desigo CC model is common to the Detection tree.
