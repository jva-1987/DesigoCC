System Overview

The Desigo CC management platform presents a single point of entry for users to operate, monitor and optimize building automation, fire safety and security systems or a combination thereof.

Desigo CC is a flexible, full client-server architecture allowing scalability from small and medium to large and complex systems. The platform provides customizable and market-specific distributions.

Desigo CC can be installed on one single computer, with full server and client functionality. Furthermore, Installed, Web and Windows App Clients can also be added on separate hardware.

Additional system connections can be made through systems installed with Front End Processors (FEP) configurations.

Web interfaces provide the customer an increased flexibility for operation and future extensions, for example, mobile applications for tablets and smart phones.
