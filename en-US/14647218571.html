Upgrading CS11 AlgoRex Fire Systems

Upgrading Desigo CC from V2.x to V3.0

When you upgrade Desigo CC from V2.x/V3.0 to V4.0, just follow the standard Desigo CC upgrade procedure.

In addition, to profit of the new Digital Zone customization option, you need to re-import the CS11 configuration. In V4.0, technical zones (structure 1610, type 30) are assigned to the new Digital Zone object model. By default, the technical zones have the same behavior as extinguishing sprinkler zones.
