Weather Forecast Graphic Templates

The following graphic templates are provided with the installation of the Weather Forecast extension common library. For details on the different properties, see Weather Forecast Object Models.

TEM_WF_Day_None_001_150

In Engineering mode, the day graphic template displays how weather forecasts are graphically represented in a day for a location.
