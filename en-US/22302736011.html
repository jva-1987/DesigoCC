Discovery Info

The Discovery Info expander displays information about the default BACnet device discovery settings, which you can modify.

Discovery Info

Item

Description

Enable Auto Discovery

Indicates that objects will be automatically discovered for this device as they are added.

Disable Consistency Alarms

Prevents a device from displaying inconsistency alarms from the database.

Disable Full Discovery

Prevents a device from doing a full discovery (device objects will not be discovered).
