Migrating or Upgrading SiPass

You can directly upgrade to V7 from V5.0 or higher. To upgrade from older Desigo CC versions, you must upgrade through intermediate steps. See the instructions below.

NOTE: The Danger Management Propagation extension is no longer supported in Desigo CC starting from V7. If you are upgrading a project that included this extension, you must remove the extension either before or immediately after upgrading. In addition, any macros, reactions or scripts that relied on propagated properties will have to be reworked.

Upgrade Desigo CC with SiPass to V7

  • From Desigo CC V5.x or V6.x, just follow the standard Desigo CC upgrade procedure for V7.
  • From older versions, you must first upgrade to V5.x or V6.x (see instructions below), and then follow the standard Desigo CC upgrade procedure for V7.

Upgrade Desigo CC with SiPass to V5.x or V6.x.

  • From Desigo CC V4.x, follow the standard Desigo CC upgrade procedure for V5.x or V6.x .
  • From Desigo CC V3.x, follow the standard Desigo CC upgrade procedure for V5.x.
  • From Desigo CC V2.x, you must first upgrade to V3.x or V4.x by uninstalling and reinstalling the software (refer to instructions below). Then you can follow the standard Desigo CC upgrade procedure for V5.x or V6.x.

Upgrade Desigo CC V2.x with SiPass to V3.x or V4.x

  1. Uninstall Desigo CC V2.x.
    NOTE: The upgrade of Desigo CC with SiPass is not supported. You must uninstall the previous version of the Desigo CC software.
  2. Install Desigo CC V3.x or V4.x with the Security > SiPass extension.
  3. Restore the V2.x project.
  4. Ignore the V2.x SiPass objects in the project and instead configure a new SiPass driver, network, and server, and import the SiPass configuration into it. For instructions, see the workflow Integrating SiPass Access Control.
  5. Contact customer support about how to delete the V2.x configuration objects (you need to apply a special procedure that is not performed automatically).
