OZW Events

The following is the list of events available in Desigo CC for the OZW integration:

  • Main Device Connection Fault
  • Subdevice Fault
  • Subdevice Room Fault
  • MainDevice Energy Indicator Active
  • Subdevice Energy Indicator Active
  • SubdeviceRoom Energy Indicator Active
  • GroupContainer Last Error
  • Scheduler Last Error
  • Calendar Last Error
