Manually Overriding the Light Fixture Profile Setting

  1. The light fixture was assigned a profile. See Setting Light Fixture Profile.
  1. In System Browser, select Project > Field Networks > [SORIS network] > [Enlighted adapter] > [energy manager] > [campus] > [building] > [floor] > [area] > [light fixture].
  2. In the Extended Operation tab, next to the Dimming Value property, enter the dimming percentage and click Manual Override.
    NOTE: The manual override lasts until you set the Auto Mode for the light fixture or you set the emergency lighting for the area or the energy manager.
