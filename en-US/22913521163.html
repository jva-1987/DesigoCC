Create Plant Operation

  1. The group title "Plant control" is created.
  1. In System Browser, select logical view.
  2. Select Logical > [network name] >...> [MyAhu].OpModMan.
  3. Drag the plant point object to Row 01 Reference.
  4. In Row 01 Type, enter the value 1 for multistate object.
